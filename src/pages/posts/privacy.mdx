## Chess Together Privacy Policy

This privacy statement applies when you play Chess Together.

**Kane Rogers does not collect your personal information when you play Chess Together.** There are no user accounts in Chess Together. Game data is non-personally identifying information, such as the moves you make. We do not link your game data to other personal information that may be stored on your device.

Your game data is stored on your device. It may also be uploaded to Google Firebase to enable you to play in multiplayer mode. You can read more about Google Firebase here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy. Limited game data, for example when you open the app, you start or end a match, or key in-game events occur, may be uploaded to Unity Analytics.
